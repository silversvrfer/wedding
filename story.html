Welcome to OUR STORY

If you have landed on this page, you may be curious on how we met. We first met in highschool but we were not your typical highschool sweethearts because most of our 14-year relationship were spent apart. Your reactions may be huh? Wait? Paano nangyari yun? Ito na nga haha. The year was 2008 in Riyadh, Saudi Arabia. It was when Yahoo Messenger is where you slide your DMs, with all its Emoticons and the “BUZZ” button HAHA. Renson had to move back to Philippines in the summer of 2009 to go to college, leaving Jhoennyne to continue her highschool. It was not easy. Being in LDR, we were forced to communicate through chat and video calls while managing our different time zones. That experience made our communication stronger. Then from 2011-2013, we were both studying in UST. In 2014, we were about 4-5 years into the relationship so you’d think “Finally magkasama naman na sila”, but Renson had to work abroad so back to LDR. Now, we meet once a year whenever he comes back for a vacation. But we are hopeful that soon we will finally be together. If you want more details, ask us during the wedding!
